JOB DESCRIPTION — DATA SCIENTIST

Job Title: Data Scientist
Department: Analytics & AI
Location: Bangalore / Remote
Employment Type: Full-time

Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled Data Scientist to join our Analytics & AI team. The ideal candidate has strong analytical skills, expertise in machine learning, and experience working with large datasets to derive meaningful insights. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams including engineering, product, and business stakeholders to build scalable data-driven solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

Analyze large structured and unstructured datasets to extract valuable insights.

Build, train, and deploy machine learning and statistical models for classification, regression, forecasting, NLP, and clustering problems.

Perform feature engineering, data cleansing, data preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis (EDA).

Collaborate with engineering teams to design and implement data pipelines and model deployment workflows.

Translate business requirements into technical data solutions.

Present insights, results, and recommendations to stakeholders through dashboards, reports, and visualizations.

Optimize models using hyperparameter tuning, cross-validation, and performance metrics.

Work with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) to manage large-scale data environments.

Conduct A/B tests, evaluate experiments, and monitor model performance in production.

Stay updated with the latest advancements in machine learning, deep learning, and data engineering.

Required Skills and Qualifications:

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, or a related field.

2–5 years of experience working as a Data Scientist or related role.

Strong knowledge of Python and data science libraries (NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn).

Experience with machine learning algorithms including regression, decision trees, random forests, gradient boosting, SVM, clustering, and neural networks.

Experience with SQL and data querying.

Hands-on knowledge of model deployment using tools such as Flask, FastAPI, Docker, or cloud ML services.

Experience with deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch is a plus.

Familiarity with NLP techniques and libraries such as NLTK, SpaCy, or transformers is beneficial.

Experience working with cloud environments like AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure.

Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications:

Experience performing end-to-end ML lifecycle including MLOps.

Familiarity with big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop, Databricks).

Experience in designing scalable data pipelines.

Understanding of CI/CD pipelines and automated model evaluation systems.

What We Offer:

Competitive salary and performance bonuses.

Opportunity to work on cutting-edge data science projects.

Collaborative and innovative work environment.

Career development and learning opportunities.

Flexible remote work options.
